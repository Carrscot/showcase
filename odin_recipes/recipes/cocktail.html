Old Fashioned

Description

The one that started it all. This classic cocktail is said to be one of the first cocktails ever made and is still enjoyed to this day. Boasting strong citrus flavours this classic bourbon cocktail has a lot of depth and flavour for the simplicity of it's ingredients.

This cocktail is simple with very few ingredients and requires no special equipment making it very accesible to people new to making cocktails. A must have for any whiskey or bourbon lover or for anyone who is interested in getting into whiskey but finds straight whikey a bit intimidating. Great on its own or paired with salty snacks and foods.

Ingredients

  • 25ml or simple syrup (or1/2 teaspoon of sugar)
  • Angostora bitters
  • 50ml of good bourbon (Woodford Reserve for this recipe)
  • 1 fresh oragne
  • Premade ice block

Preparation Instructions

  1. Add a large premade ice block into a tumbler of your choosing.
  2. Add 25ml of simple syrup (adjust this according to how sweet you would like your cocktail but 25ml is the maximum recommended amount).
  3. Add 50 ml of your favourite bourbon or whiskey. (Woodford Reserve in this case)
  4. Add 3 to 5 dashed of Angostorabitters.
  5. Stir mixture in glass until everything is incorporated.
  6. Peel a large peel of orange and squeeze the oils into the glass.
  7. Rub the orange peel around the rim of the glass.
  8. Garnish with the orange peel and enjoy!
